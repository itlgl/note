title: Android设置TextView可以上下和左右同时滑动
date: 2018-03-12 10:28:35 
categories: 
- note
tags: 
- Android
---

布局文件：
```xml
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?>
<GridLayout
        xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android"
        android:layout_width="match_parent"
        android:layout_height="match_parent"
        android:rowCount="2"
        android:columnCount="1"
        android:paddingTop="10sp"
        android:paddingLeft="10sp">

    <TextView
            android:layout_width="wrap_content"
            android:layout_height="wrap_content"
            android:text="@string/last_log_entries"
            android:layout_gravity="left"
            />

    <TextView
            android:id="@+id/tvLogDisplay"
            android:layout_width="fill_parent"
            android:layout_height="fill_parent"
            android:fontFamily="monospace"
            android:textSize="14sp"
            android:scrollbars="horizontal|vertical"
            android:padding="5sp"/>

</GridLayout>
```

设置的代码：
```java
TextView tvLogDisplay = (TextView) findViewById(R.id.tvLogDisplay);
tvLogDisplay.setText(logs);
tvLogDisplay.setHorizontallyScrolling(true);
tvLogDisplay.setMovementMethod(new ScrollingMovementMethod());
```
